How to Play

  • Select a ship from the fleet panel
  • Choose a grid position (A1-J10)
  • Select orientation: Horizontal or Vertical
  • Click "Place Ship" to position your ship
  • Place all 5 ships to start the game
  • Click on a placed ship to remove it
